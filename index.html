My family and I have seen a lot of the United States. Most of our earlier trips were planned by where my husband traveled for business. Now, they are planned by where our children live. In the past six years, we have seen California, Hawaii, and Nevada courtesy of our oldest son. He is in the Navy. Cleveland has become one of our top tourist destinations since our oldest daughter moved there several years ago. This year, we have added Georgia to our list. Our youngest son joined the Army and is stationed near Savannah.
